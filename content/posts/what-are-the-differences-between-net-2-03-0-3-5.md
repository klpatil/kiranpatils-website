In short:
**2.0** =\> framework that shipped with VS 2005 VB 8.0 / C# 2.0
**3.0** =\> same framework as 2.0 + WCF + WPF + WF
**3.5** =\> all the above + LINQ technologies and will ship with the next VS including VB 9.0 and C#
